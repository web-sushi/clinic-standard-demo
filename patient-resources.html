Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request an appointment?

You can request an appointment through our contact form or by calling our clinic directly.

Do you accept new patients?

Yes, we are currently accepting new patients. Availability may vary depending on services requested.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Please bring a valid ID, insurance card (if applicable), and any relevant medical records.

How will I receive test results?

Our clinic will contact you to discuss results and next steps once they are available.
